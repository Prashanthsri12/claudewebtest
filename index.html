About Us

Trusted Care in the Heart of Mogappair

Diagnostics

Comprehensive Diagnostic Services

State-of-the-art diagnostic tools for accurate, timely results — all under one roof.

Sleep Study

Detailed analysis of sleep disorders and patterns.

EEG

Electroencephalogram for brain activity monitoring.

EMG

Electromyography for muscle & nerve evaluation.

NCS

Nerve Conduction Study for peripheral neuropathy.

BiPAP / CPAP

Non-invasive ventilation therapy for sleep apnea.

24Hr Ambulatory BP Monitor

Round-the-clock blood pressure tracking and analysis.

Pulmonary Function Test

Assessment of lung capacity and breathing efficiency.

24Hr Holter Monitor

Continuous ECG recording to detect cardiac arrhythmias.

Audiometry

Comprehensive hearing assessment and audiological testing.
