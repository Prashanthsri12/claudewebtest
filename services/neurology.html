About This Service

Comprehensive Neurological Expertise

Dynamic Creative Health Care's Neurology department offers specialist-level care for the full range of neurological conditions, from common headaches and epilepsy to complex movement disorders and neurodegenerative diseases. Dr. K. Sankara Subramanian — a qualified DM Neurologist — brings extensive sub-specialist expertise to every case, ensuring precise diagnosis and tailored management plans.

Our neurological services are supported by advanced diagnostic equipment including EEG, EMG, and nerve conduction studies, enabling us to investigate and confirm diagnoses with confidence. We believe in a thorough, patient-centered approach that addresses not only the condition itself but also its impact on quality of life, daily function, and long-term outcomes.

  • Epilepsy & seizure management
  • Headache & migraine treatment
  • Stroke diagnosis & rehabilitation
  • Sleep disorder treatment
  • Neuromuscular disease care

Conditions We Treat

From epilepsy and migraines to Parkinson's and dementia — we provide expert care for the complete spectrum of neurological disorders.

Epilepsy & Seizures

EEG-guided seizure management and medication optimization.

Headaches & Migraines

Diagnosis and preventive migraine therapy.

Stroke

Acute management and long-term rehabilitation planning.

Sleep Disorders

Sleep study, insomnia, sleep apnea diagnosis & treatment.

Parkinson's Disease

Tremor management and movement disorder care.

Dementia

Cognitive assessment and memory disorder management.

Spine Disorders

Cervical/lumbar disc disease, myelopathy management.

Peripheral Neuropathy

NCS/EMG-guided nerve damage diagnosis and treatment.

Multiple Sclerosis

Diagnosis, disease modification and symptom management.

Neurological Diagnostic Arsenal

State-of-the-art investigations to ensure accurate diagnosis and optimal treatment planning.

EEG

Electroencephalography for seizure detection and brain activity mapping.

EMG

Electromyography to assess muscle and motor neuron function.

NCS

Nerve conduction study for peripheral nerve and neuropathy evaluation.

Sleep Study

Polysomnography and sleep analysis for sleep apnea and insomnia diagnosis.

BiPAP / CPAP

Non-invasive ventilatory support for sleep-disordered breathing.

24 Hr Holter

Ambulatory cardiac monitoring to detect arrhythmias related to neurological events.
