About This Service

Comprehensive Medical Care for Every Condition

At Dynamic Creative Health Care, our General & Emergency Medicine department provides a full spectrum of primary healthcare services for patients of all ages. Led by Dr. R. Prithviraj — a specialist in both General and Emergency Medicine — we deliver evidence-based care with a personal touch, ensuring every patient receives a thorough assessment and a clear treatment plan.

From managing acute infections and chronic diseases to providing round-the-clock emergency stabilization, our team is equipped to handle the complete range of medical needs. We emphasize early diagnosis, lifestyle counseling, and preventive screenings to keep your health ahead of illness rather than just reacting to it.

  • Acute illness management
  • Chronic disease care
  • Health screenings & preventive check-ups
  • Emergency stabilization & triage
  • Preventive care & lifestyle counseling

Conditions We Treat

We diagnose and manage a broad range of medical conditions, from everyday ailments to complex chronic diseases and emergencies.

Fever & Infections

Viral, bacterial, and parasitic infections including typhoid and dengue.

Respiratory Illness

Asthma, bronchitis, pneumonia, COPD management.

Diabetes

Blood sugar management, complications monitoring and prevention.

Hypertension

Blood pressure management and cardiovascular risk reduction.

Gastrointestinal

Acidity, IBS, liver conditions, abdominal pain workup.

Allergies

Allergic rhinitis, urticaria, skin reactions and management.

Musculoskeletal

Joint pain, arthritis, back pain, sports injuries.

Emergency Care

Trauma assessment, wound care, emergency stabilization.

Preventive Care

Vaccinations, health check-ups, lifestyle counseling.
