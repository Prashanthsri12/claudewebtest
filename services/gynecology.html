About This Service

Compassionate Women's Healthcare

At Prithvi Clinic, our Gynecology & Obstetrics department offers comprehensive women's health services delivered with expertise and compassion. Under the care of Dr. S.V. Priyanka — an experienced obstetrician and gynecologist — we provide evidence-based care tailored to each woman's unique needs, from adolescence through menopause and beyond.

Whether you are planning a family, navigating a high-risk pregnancy, managing a hormonal condition such as PCOS, or seeking regular gynecological screening, our team ensures you receive personalized, dignified, and thorough medical attention in a supportive environment. We combine the latest clinical practices with a deep respect for every patient's concerns.

  • Prenatal & antenatal care
  • High-risk pregnancy management
  • Gynecological surgeries
  • PCOS / PCOD treatment
  • Menstrual disorder management

Conditions We Treat

Comprehensive women's health services covering obstetric, gynecological, and hormonal conditions at every life stage.

Prenatal Care

Complete antenatal care and pregnancy monitoring.

High-Risk Pregnancy

Specialized monitoring for complex pregnancies.

PCOS / PCOD

Hormonal management and fertility support.

Menstrual Disorders

Irregular cycles, heavy bleeding, dysmenorrhea.

Cervical Health

Pap smear, HPV screening, colposcopy.

Infertility

Initial workup and fertility counseling.

Menopause

Hormone therapy and symptom management.

Gynecological Infections

UTI, vaginitis, STI screening and treatment.

Surgical Procedures

Laparoscopy, D&C, fibroid management.
