Pharmacy at Home

Skip the queue at the pharmacy. Dynamic Creative Health Care delivers your prescribed medications and over-the-counter products directly to your home across Mogappair and nearby areas. Our pharmacy team verifies every prescription and ensures medications are safely packaged and delivered on time.

Investigations at Home
Service 02

Investigations at Home

Our trained phlebotomists and healthcare technicians visit your home to collect samples for blood tests, urine analysis, and perform bedside investigations such as ECG and glucose monitoring. All samples are processed at accredited laboratories with reports delivered directly to you.

Home investigations are ideal for elderly patients, post-operative care, individuals with mobility challenges, or anyone who simply values convenience without compromising on quality and accuracy of results.

  • Blood tests & CBC panels
  • Urine analysis & culture
  • ECG at home
  • Glucose monitoring & HbA1c
  • Safe & sterile sample collection
  • Digital report delivery

Medical Equipment at Home

We supply and deliver a wide range of medical equipment for home use, whether for short-term post-operative recovery or long-term chronic care management. All equipment is sanitised, calibrated, and ready for immediate use upon delivery.

Our team provides full setup assistance and user training so patients and caregivers feel confident operating the equipment. We also offer rental and purchase options to suit different care needs and budgets.

  • Oxygen concentrators
  • Wheelchairs & mobility aids
  • Hospital beds & mattresses
  • BP monitors & glucometers
  • Pulse oximeters
  • Nebulizers & spacers
