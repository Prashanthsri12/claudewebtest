Why Accurate Diagnosis Matters

Getting the right diagnosis is the single most important step in your healthcare journey. A precise diagnosis allows your doctor to craft a targeted treatment plan, avoiding unnecessary medications or procedures that could delay recovery and increase costs.

Early and accurate diagnostic testing can reveal underlying conditions before they progress to serious illness. From neurological disorders to cardiac irregularities and respiratory conditions, timely detection saves lives and improves long-term outcomes.

  • Accurate diagnosis tailored to your unique condition
  • Faster, targeted treatment with fewer complications
  • Preventive insights to safeguard your long-term health

What We Offer

Our Diagnostic Services

Comprehensive testing capabilities to investigate neurological, cardiac, respiratory, and auditory conditions with precision.

Sleep Study

A comprehensive overnight sleep analysis to diagnose sleep disorders including sleep apnea, insomnia, restless leg syndrome, and narcolepsy. Our sleep studies are conducted using advanced polysomnography equipment.

  • Overnight monitoring
  • Brain & eye activity recording
  • Respiratory monitoring
  • Oxygen saturation tracking

EEG (Electroencephalogram)

Electroencephalography measures and records the electrical activity of the brain. Used primarily to diagnose epilepsy, brain tumors, head injuries, and sleep disorders.

  • Epilepsy diagnosis
  • Brain activity mapping
  • Seizure localization
  • Routine & ambulatory EEG

EMG (Electromyography)

Electromyography evaluates the health of muscles and the nerve cells (motor neurons) that control them. Essential for diagnosing neuromuscular diseases.

  • Muscle disease diagnosis
  • Nerve function testing
  • ALS & myopathy workup
  • Post-stroke assessment

NCS (Nerve Conduction Study)

Nerve Conduction Studies measure the speed and strength of electrical signals through nerves. Critical for diagnosing peripheral neuropathy, carpal tunnel, and Guillain-Barré.

  • Peripheral neuropathy
  • Carpal tunnel syndrome
  • Radiculopathy
  • Polyneuropathy evaluation

BiPAP / CPAP Therapy

Bilevel and Continuous Positive Airway Pressure therapy for patients with obstructive sleep apnea and respiratory conditions. We provide both titration and ongoing therapy support.

  • Sleep apnea treatment
  • COPD management
  • Home setup support
  • Mask fitting & training

24Hr Ambulatory BP Monitor

A portable blood pressure monitor worn for 24 hours to track BP fluctuations during daily activities and sleep — the gold standard for hypertension diagnosis.

  • White coat hypertension
  • Masked hypertension
  • BP variability analysis
  • Medication efficacy monitoring

Pulmonary Function Test (PFT)

Spirometry and comprehensive lung function testing to assess breathing capacity. Used for diagnosing asthma, COPD, restrictive lung disease, and pre-surgical assessment.

  • FEV1/FVC measurement
  • Bronchodilator response
  • Diffusion capacity
  • Pre-surgical lung clearance

24Hr Holter Monitor

A wearable ECG device that continuously records heart rhythm for 24 hours, detecting arrhythmias, palpitations, and silent cardiac events missed by standard ECG.

  • Arrhythmia detection
  • Palpitation evaluation
  • Heart block diagnosis
  • Post-syncope workup

Audiometry

Comprehensive hearing assessment using pure-tone and speech audiometry to evaluate hearing sensitivity across frequencies. Used for hearing loss diagnosis and hearing aid fitting.

  • Pure-tone audiometry
  • Speech audiometry
  • Tympanometry
  • Hearing aid assessment
